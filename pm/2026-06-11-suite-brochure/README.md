# Weft — the suite brochure

**What this is:** marketing copy for the Weft federation — one page on the
foundation and its philosophy, one detailed page per member, plus the extras
and the potentials. This is *ad copy, not reference*: it sells the story.
Every factual claim is grounded in the doctrine and member briefings as of
2026-06-11, but versions, tool counts, and surface shapes are deliberately
omitted — the repos own those.

**Audience:** developers running coding agents (solo or fleets) who are
deciding whether to adopt the suite; secondarily, anyone evaluating the
federation's design philosophy.
